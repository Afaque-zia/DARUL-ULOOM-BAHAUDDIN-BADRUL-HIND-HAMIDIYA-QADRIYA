DARUL-ULOOM BAHAUDDIN BADRUL-HIND HAMIDIYA QADRIYA

Darul-Uloom Bahauddin Badrul-Hind Hamidiya Qadriya is a center of Islamic learning established with the vision and guidance of Ulama-e-Akabireen to serve Deen and provide high-quality religious and moral education. The aim is to nurture a new generation of students who are deeply rooted in Islamic values and equipped to face modern-day challenges.
The institution offers a wide range of courses including:

  • Dars-e-Nizami
  • Hifz-ul-Quran
  • Tajweed and Qira’at
  • Education up to Aaliya level (recognized by Madrasa Board)

We are proud to maintain

A team of well-qualified, ethical, and dedicated teachers (Maulimeen and Muallimaat)
A clean and peaceful environment conducive to learning
Regular Islahi (reformative) gatherings and special sessions for intellectual guidance
Excellent boarding and food facilities

In addition to Islamic studies

students are also taught:

Hindi

English

Mathematics

Tailoring

Embroidery


There is also a special arrangement for Islamic education for girls, ensuring equal access to Deeni taleem in a safe and separate setup.
